Thank you for your interest in attending a Madison Percussion Academy MPA MadChops Experience (MCX)© event! The Madison Percussion Academy is hosting 2 pre-season open clinics in October, culminating in our 3 call-back camps. The MadChops Experience (MCX) Open Clinics will focus on technique and is open to any percussionist wanting to improve their skills, whether or not they're planning to audition for the ensemble. The pre-audition call-back camps will focus on the MPA|2017 audition materials and are intended for those percussionists looking for a spot with MPA|2017, but are still open for anyone who wants to attend.

Audition Online

Although there is no Audition Fee for the MadChops Experience in 2017, all participants will pay a Facility Usage Fee of $15 for each event. The fee covers the use of the facility.

Registration Form


All clinics/auditions will be at James Clemens High School Band Room at 11306 County Line Rd, Madison AL 35756.

Materials Needed: Battery players bring your drum and sticks, front ensemble players bring your own mallets, and drumset players bring small drumsticks.
